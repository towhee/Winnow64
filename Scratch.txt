Height of thumbDock (ht) changes
    If thumbDock top or bottom
        Calc new thumbSpace to fit
        Calc maxThumbs fit without scrollBar
        If scrollBar req'd and no scrollBar
            newHt = ht + scrollBarHt
            Resize thumbDock to newHt - causes recurse to #1
        If scrollBar not req'd and is scrollBar
            newHt = ht - scrollBarHt
            Resize thumbDock to newHt - causes recurse to #1
        Resize thumbs to fit
