Performance Tips

If you are experiencing a delay between selecting an image and it being displayed then try the following:

Disable color management

To keep the correct colors, in your camera, set to use sRGB. This will only affect the previews, not the raw data.

Disable combine raw/jpg

Unless you need it for other purposes, turn 'Save Previews' off in your camera. The preview will still be embedded in the raw file, which is what Winnow uses.

Disable caching progress

In Preferences > Cache: turn off 'Show caching progress'. This will have a small impact on performance.
